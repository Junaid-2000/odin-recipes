Pasta

Below you can find the ingredients and the instructions to make this delicious meal

Ingredients

Pasta

  • Pasta (spaghetii, penne, fusilli)
  • Water
  • Salt

Red Sauce

  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Puree
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Chilli Flakes
  • Sugar

Instructions

  1. Cook the Pasta
    • Bring a large pot of water to a boil
    • Add salt and taste
    • Add Pasta
    • Drain and set aside (Save pasta water)
  2. Make Red Sauce
    • Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil
    • Add chopped garlic
    • Pour in tomato puree
    • Stir and let it cook on medium hear for 8 minutes
  3. Season The Sauce
    • Add salt, black pepper and chilli flakes
    • Add dried herbs oregano and basil
    • Add a small pinch of sugar
    • Let the sauce simmer till it thickens slightly
  4. Combine Pasta and Sauce
    • Add the cooked pasta to the sauce
    • Toss well so the pasta is coated
    • If the sauce is too thick add some pasta water
    • Enjoy your meal :)
