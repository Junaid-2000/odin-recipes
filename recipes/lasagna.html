Lasagna

Below you can find the ingredients and the instructions to make this delicious meal

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Minced Meat
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Oil
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook The Filling
    • Heat oil in pan
    • Cook chopped onion and garlic
    • Add minced meat
    • Add tomato sauce, salt and pepper
    • Let it simmer for few minutes
  2. Make White Sauce
    • Melt butter in pan
    • Add flour and stir
    • Slowly add mill and continue stirring
    • Cook until it's smooth and thick
  3. Assemble Lasagna
    • In a baking dish spread a layer of meat sauce
    • Add layer of lasagna sheets
    • Add white sauce and cheese
    • Repeat for as many layers as you like
  4. Bake
    • Finish with cheese on top
    • Bake in oven at 180 degrees for 30 minutes until golden
    • Enjoy your meal :)
